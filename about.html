About Dr. Sagar Bhattarai

Dr. Sagar Bhattarai is a dedicated post-doctoral researcher at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati, currently ranked 364 in the QS World University Rankings 2024. His research focuses on cutting-edge advancements in photovoltaic devices, particularly in perovskite solar cells, organic light-emitting diodes, and mathematical modeling. Dr. Bhattarai has made significant contributions to the field of optoelectronics, exploring materials such as III-V inorganic compounds, quantum dots, and quantum wells.

With a Ph.D. from the National Institute of Technology (NIT) Arunachal Pradesh, where he investigated perovskite materials for solar cell applications, Dr. Bhattarai has been actively involved in both theoretical and practical research in renewable energy technologies. His academic journey also includes roles as an Assistant Professor and Head of the Physics Department at Arunachal University of Studies, India.
